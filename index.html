First José Rizal Monument

Built in 1898, the First José Rizal Monument in Daet is the earliest memorial honoring the Philippines' national hero. Constructed by local townspeople, it predates Manila's famous Rizal Monument by over a decade.

Over the years, it has remained a symbol of patriotism and pride for Daet.

LocationBarangay Mangcayo, Daet, Camarines Norte
FoundedDecember 30, 1898
FoundersLt. Colonels Antonio Sanz and Ildefonso Alegre
Historical SignificanceFirst Rizal monument in the country
ArchitectureThree-tiered stone pylon
Cultural RoleCommemoration during Rizal Day
